About

Engineer by qualification, entrepreneur by mindset, creator by design.

My work sits at the intersection of mechanical design, product thinking, manufacturing practicality, and clear communication. I care about building products that are technically sound, visually clean, easy to manufacture, and useful in the real world.

I bring 500+ hours of hands-on CAD modeling, 2D drafting, and documentation practice across client work and personal product projects. Alongside design execution, I focus on client alignment, stakeholder communication, project clarity, and business viability.
